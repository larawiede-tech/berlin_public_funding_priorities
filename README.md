# Berlin Public Funding Priorities (2020–2024)
-- ------------------------------------------------------
Project Overview
-- -------------------------------------------------------
This project analyzes how public funding priorities evolved in Berlin between 2020 and 2024 using more than 57,000 grant records from the Berlin Open Data Portal.

The objective is to identify funding trends across policy areas and explore how public resource allocation changed during the post-pandemic period.
-- -------------------------------------------------------
Research Question
-- -------------------------------------------------------
How did Berlin's public funding priorities evolve between 2020 and 2024?
-- -------------------------------------------------------
Dataset
-- -------------------------------------------------------
Source: Berlin Open Data Portal

- 57,469 grant records
- Period 2020–2024
- 34 policy areas
- Institutional and project funding
-- -------------------------------------------------------
Key variables:
-- -------------------------------------------------------
- Funding authority (geber)
- Funding type (art)
- Year (jahr)
- Policy area (politikbereich)
- Project purpose (zweck)
- Funding amount (betrag)
- Recipient organization (name)

-- -------------------------------------------------------
Preliminary Findings
-- -------------------------------------------------------
- Economic funding (Wirtschaft) increased substantially between 2020 and 2024.
- Employment-related funding (Arbeit) lost relative importance over the same period.
- Research funding (Forschung) gained importance in Berlin's funding portfolio.
- Urban Development (Stadtentwicklung) experienced the largest decline in funding share.
- Transport funding (Verkehr) also decreased between 2020 and 2024.
